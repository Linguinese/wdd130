Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this rafting website is to establish an online presence for Adventure Rapids Rafting, to provide potential customers with information about our rafting trips, safety measures, and booking options.

Audience

The target audience for the Adventure Rapids Rafting website includes:

  • Adventure seekers looking for thrilling outdoor experiences.
  • Families planning vacations and looking for fun, safe activities for all ages.
  • Corporate groups seeking team-building exercises and group adventures.
Scenario Questions:
  1. Are you an adventure enthusiast looking for a new exciting activity?
    Yes, I'm always looking for new outdoor adventures.
  2. Are you planning a family vacation and looking for safe activities suitable for children?
    Yes, we want activities that everyone in the family can enjoy safely.
  3. Is your company looking for team-building activities that promote teamwork and excitement?
    Yes, we are searching for activities that foster team spirit and are enjoyable for all employees.
